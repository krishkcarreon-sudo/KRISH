HAUNTED FARM 3D

Survive the haunted night. When dawn comes, find the 🔑 ESCAPE KEY and reach the FRONT GATE — or defeat The Farmer!

Use the farm to survive longer and move smarter.
Crouch and press F to crawl through vents and slip between rooms. Most animatronics cannot follow you there.
You can hide under certain tables while crouching, and hide inside lockers to break enemy pursuit.
Find batteries around the farm to restore your flashlight power back to 100%.
Find gas cans and take them to the generator room to refill power.
Visit the workshop to learn the story and revive your dog with a bone.
The farmhouse kitchen lets you gather ingredients and cook meals for healing.
